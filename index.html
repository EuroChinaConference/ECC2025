Welcome to the 9th Euro-China Conference on Intelligent Data Analysis and Applications (ECC 2025), hosted by VSB-Technical University Ostrava and Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology. Join us in Ostrava, Czech Republic, from July 21–23, 2025, for an internationally respected forum on intelligent data analysis, computational intelligence, signal processing, and AI applications.
