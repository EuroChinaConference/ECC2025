Instructions for Paper Submission

We are pleased to announce that paper submission for ECC 2025 will be managed through the Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit (CMT) system. Kindly follow the instructions below to register and submit your paper.

Step 1: Register on CMT

Before submitting your paper, you must create an account on the CMT platform:

  1. Visit the CMT registration page: Register Here.
  2. Click on "Register" in the top-right corner of the page.
  3. Fill in the required details, including your email address, name, and affiliation.
  4. Submit the registration form and verify your email to activate your account.

If you already have a CMT account, you can skip this step.

Step 2: Submit Your Paper

Once registered, follow these steps to submit your paper:

  1. Log in to your CMT account.
  2. Go to the ECC 2025 submission site: Paper Submission Link (https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ECCconf2025).
  3. Complete the required fields, upload your paper, and confirm your submission.

Please ensure that your submission adheres to the guidelines provided on the ECC 2025 conference website.

Step 3: Registration

The registration process consists of two steps: completing the form and making the payment-direct to https://karty.vsb.cz/.

  1. Payment: Register and Pay

    For overseas authors and participants, please complete the registration and payment as outlined below. At least one author of each accepted paper must register and pay the fee by June 15, 2025. The registration fee is $550 per person (payment must be made in advance). Discounts may be available for those with funding constraints.

    The registration fee includes:

    • Conference proceedings
    • Conference program guide
    • Access to all technical sessions
    • Lunches and dinners
    • Coffee breaks
    • Conference gifts

    Note: Travel and lodging expenses are not included.

  2. Registration Form: Complete the Registration Form

Please fill out the registration form above.
