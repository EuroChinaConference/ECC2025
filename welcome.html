Welcome to ECC 2025

The 9th Euro-China Conference on Intelligent Data Analysis and Applications (ECC 2025), hosted by VSB-Technical University Ostrava and Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology, is a premier international forum for cutting-edge research in intelligent data analysis, computational intelligence, signal processing, and AI applications.

We invite submissions of high-quality original research papers on topics including machine learning, data mining, AI-driven signal processing, and more. Join us to connect with global researchers and innovators!

Step: Registration

The registration process consists of two steps: completing the form then you will get the payment link by email. The email link redirected to our secure payment portal at eps.vsb.cz for fast and secure payment by credit card.

  1. Registration Form: Complete the Registration Form

    After registration, you will receive a payment link via email to complete the payment process at eps.vsb.cz. Ensure payment is completed by June 15, 2025.

    For all authors and participants, please complete the registration and payment by June 15, 2025. The registration fee is $550 per paper (12,000 CZK or 480 EUR). Discounts may be available for those with funding constraints and write to meenu.singh@vsb.cz.

    The registration fee includes:

    • Conference proceedings
    • Conference program guide
    • Access to all technical sessions
    • Lunches and dinners
    • Coffee breaks
    • Conference gifts

    Note: Travel and lodging expenses are not included.
